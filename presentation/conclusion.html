Conclusion & Future Work

Key Findings

The AI-driven traffic control system demonstrated a 40% reduction in average waiting time compared to traditional fixed-time systems.
Vehicle speed improved by 13% on average, indicating better traffic flow and reduced congestion.
The YOLO-based vehicle detection system achieved 92% accuracy in identifying and classifying vehicles in various lighting conditions.
Limitations: The physical prototype requires further optimization for real-world deployment, and the system needs more extensive testing with larger traffic networks.

Integration with smart city infrastructure and IoT devices for more comprehensive data collection.
