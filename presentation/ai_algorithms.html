AI Algorithms

Core AI Components

Adaptive Signal Control: Uses reinforcement learning to optimize traffic signal timing based on real-time traffic conditions, minimizing waiting times and maximizing throughput.
Dynamic Routing: Implements graph-based algorithms to calculate optimal routes considering current traffic density, incidents, and predicted congestion patterns.
Incident Detection: Uses anomaly detection algorithms to identify traffic incidents, accidents, or unusual congestion patterns requiring intervention.
Computer Vision: YOLOv5 model for vehicle detection, classification, and counting from camera feeds with 92% accuracy on the test dataset.
